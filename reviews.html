Cook's Illustrated

  • Service: 5/5
  • Quality: 4/5

Do you like noods? Look no further than Noods To Go, a noodle delivery shop that delivers noods directly to you! An ingenious idea cooked up by four friends, Noods To Go is a revolutionary noodle shop.

Upon placing my order, my noods arrived at my doorstep a short 2 minutes later. The noodles were packaged carefully, demonstrating Noods To Go's attention to detail.

The noodles themselves were delicious! The flavors worked so well together, blending to create the perfect taste in my mouth.

While the selection is very diverse, I would have liked to see more dishes available to order. I know I'll be ordering from here a lot, and I'd like to be able to try many new foods!

Overall, Noods To Go is a promising establishment. Go get some noods today!
